Our project

We are Aidan and Ruben, two high school students passionate about technology. For a project in SNT, we need to create a website dedicated to artificial intelligence and humanoids. Our goal is to share our interest in these innovations.

On our site, we will cover various topics related to AI: how it works, its applications in daily life, its opportunities for the future, and the dangers it may pose. Additionally, we will explore the fascinating world of humanoids, robots that mimic human appearance and behavior, examining their recent developments and potential roles in society.

This project is an opportunity to learn how to code and discover new things. We hope our site will become an enriching and accessible platform for everyone interested in these captivating topics. We are fortunate to be supported by our mathematics teacher, Mr. Morel, who guides and advises us to succeed in this challenge. Here's his website to learn more about him.
